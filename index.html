About

I'm a second-year Mechanical Engineering student at IIT Tirupati, driven by an obsession with building things that work — not just on paper, but in the real world, in real conditions, for real people.

My work spans the boundary between physical systems and digital intelligence. From AI wearables that monitor rural health to FDM solvers for battery thermal runaway, I approach every problem with the same rigour: deep research, first-principles thinking, and a relentless standard for execution.

I follow the startup and VC ecosystem closely, hold certifications from IIT Kharagpur and IISc Bangalore, and believe the best engineers are those who build AND understand why they're building.
