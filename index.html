2025 Research · Simulation

Battery Thermal Runaway

2D transient FDM solver in MATLAB simulating lithium-ion battery thermal runaway propagation across a 4×4 cell pack with adaptive air-gap stepping.

Challenge

Thermal runaway in Li-ion batteries is a cascade failure with potentially catastrophic consequences — understanding its propagation is critical for safe battery design.

Solution

Explicit FTCS scheme on a 150×150 grid with active heat source modelling, 2D vs 1D deviation quantification, and safety window analysis.

Impact

Demonstrates that 2D spatial modelling captures propagation dynamics that 1D models miss, with direct design implications for EV battery packs.

MATLABFDMEnergy2DHeat Transfer
